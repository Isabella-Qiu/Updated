Aileen Iniguez - Data Science

Data science has built an increasingly important reputation for itself, and as explained by Ms. Iniguez, it is vital in solving business problems using computer models. By using STEM skills through a business lens, a data scientist can collaborate with others on how to compile the most accurate data, then use that data to create smart, efficient plans for businesses big and small. Personally, I have always had interest in collaborative jobs where I can work and think with other people. I love the idea of being able to incorporate business aspects into computer science, and data science seems like a field that I would want to venture more deeply into.
